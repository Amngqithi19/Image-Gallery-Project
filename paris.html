PARIS

The capital of France is known for its lavish lifestyle and the immaculate design of it's 19th-century cityscape makes it one of the most beautiful cities not just in France but also in the world. Paris is home to many places that you could visit, some are just for sightseeing and many others will have you just revel in their beauty. Paris is home to the Louvre Musuem, Eiffel Tower, the Palace of Versaille and many more.
